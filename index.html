Abubacarr Sinera

Abubacarr Sinera is a passionate and visionary youth leader from The Gambia, deeply committed to Islamic values, education, and community development. 

 He is the President of the Ambassadors of Islam Association (AIA) an organization dedicated to nurturing Islamic awareness, leadership, and youth empowerment.
Through his leadership, Abubacarr strives to inspire young Muslims to live by the principles of Islam while actively contributing to the betterment of society.

An avid learner and educator, Abubacarr studies subjects including English, Mathematics, Science, History, Civic Education, Literature, Government, Islamic Studies and ICT. His passion for learning extends beyond academics, he continuously develops skills in areas like public speaking, writing, and digital literacy.

Abubacarr is also a writer, poet, and debater, recognized under his poetic name SSDP. He has participated in writing and debate competitions, including the British Parliament Debate, where he represented his views on youth participation in national development. His writings often explore themes of solitude, self-discovery, faith, and moral strength, blending Islamic philosophy and psychology to promote reflection and personal growth. His ongoing book project, "To Be Free from Lonely and Embrace the Greatness of Alone", captures this vision in depth. Beyond his academic and literary pursuits, Abubacarr is an aspiring entrepreneur, currently planning to establish a small poultry business focusing on broilers and layers, reflecting his belief in self-reliance and productive community engagement.
